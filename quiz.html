Name where you actually are.

There are five specific patterns that keep accomplished women standing on their own plateau. Most women carry one — a few carry two. The work that resolves one does not address another, so naming yours is the first useful act.

  • 01Eight questions. Each asks about a specific moment in your life — not a feeling in general.
  • 02Answer for the version of you that shows up on a normal Tuesday. Not for the one you would like to be.
  • 03Your result is not a label. It is a diagnosis — and a precise indication of the form of private work that addresses it.
